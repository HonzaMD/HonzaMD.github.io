What is KRKAL?

Krkal is puzzle-action top-down game. With one of three different characters, you have to find your way in a maze, which is full of various traps and obstacles.

You will push stones, slide on ice, avoid stepping on mines, play with switches, wander in complex labyrinths and even set up traffic signs to take advantage of traffic monsters...
