What is KRKAL?

Krkal is a puzzle action top-down game. You control one of three characters and try to find your way through a maze, which is full of various traps and obstacles.

You will move stones, slide on ice, avoid mines, properly set various switches, wonder through mazes and maybe rearrange signs for traffic monsters...
